How I Work

I recently completed a course in Analysis and Design of User Interfaces, where I gained hands-on experience in applying key design principles such as user-centered design, heuristic evaluation, and the 3Cs (Context, Clutter-Free, and Contrast). Throughout the course, I explored the use of wireframes, personas, and mockups to guide the iterative design process and improve usability. I also learned how to evaluate and refine designs based on feedback, accessibility standards, and visual hierarchy. My design workflow now follows a structured approach: identifying user needs, prototyping solutions, and testing for clarity and interaction quality. This portfolio reflects the progression of my work and the skills I've developed through this UI design journey.
